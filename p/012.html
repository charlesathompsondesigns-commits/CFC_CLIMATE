Materials

  • Veg-tan Italian cowhide, Aspen horsehide, waxed horsehide, vachetta kangaroo, or Cypress horsehide uppers depending on pair (sourced from A&A Crack and Sons, Horween Tannery Chicago, or Maryam Tannery)
  • veg-tan calfskin lining
  • veg-tan horsebutt insole (Horween Tannery Chicago)
  • veg-tan leather soles with rubber/ceramic composite, heel tap, textured rubber, or Vibram Montagna 100 lug soles depending on pair

How it was made

Bespoke, made-to-measure; new pairs shown alongside used/patinated pairs as lifecycle evidence; one pair completed by Harry's Gold Coast Shoe Repair

Care and repair

Care

  • Brush regularly with a horsehair brush to redistribute wax/oils and remove debris
  • saddle soap and conditioner needed no more than once a year
  • soles are welted and can be resoled
  • rubber outsoles often replaceable before a full resole
  • Ives may perform some maintenance/repairs for clients

Repair route

Recommended Chicago cobblers: Shoe Hospital, Mend Atelier, Red Star Cobblers, Harry's Gold Coast Shoe Repair
